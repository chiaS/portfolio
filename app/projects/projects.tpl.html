• Read Bible

    Read Bible is a solo project, made for Franciscans in Taiwan. They already have a desktop website made with CMS, but they hope to build a mobile app to bring convenience to their users.   • Sip

    I've worked on a client project Sip with a team of four, aiming to create a real-time drink ordering application, which eliminates the need to wait in line to place your order. Simply place an order from your smartphone, and you will be notified when your drink order is ready to pick up. My focus in this project was front-end, including integrating Flux with Angular, transmitting data to/from server purely using web socket, color scheme and layout design. We drove the project to beta stage completion in just under a month. The technologies used include Node.js, PubNub, MongoDB, and Auth0 on the back-end, and Ionic, Stylus, Angular with Flux on the front-end.
